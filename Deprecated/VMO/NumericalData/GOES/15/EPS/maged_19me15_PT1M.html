Description

Direct link to the GOES 15 Magnetospheric Electron Detector (MAGED) data in CSV format as well as the energetic particle data from other GOES spacecraft. The data are sorted by year, month, spacecraft, and file type. The file names indicates the data type, spacecraft number, year, month, and resolution. For example, g15_maged_19me15_1m_20110101_20110131.csv, indicates goes 15 magnetometer data at 1 m resolution from start of January 01, 2011 to the end of January 31, 2011.
