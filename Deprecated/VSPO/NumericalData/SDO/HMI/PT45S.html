SDO/HMI Data Products at JSOC/Stanford U.

ResourceID
spase://VSPO/NumericalData/SDO/HMI/PT45S

Description

The HMI-AIA (Helioseismic and Magnetic Imager -
Atmospheric Imaging Assembly) JSOC (Joint Science Operations Center)
at Stanford University archives, and provides multiple access paths
to, a variety of SDO(Solar Dynamics Observatory)/HMI digital and
display solar images and movies. Included are dopplergrams (maps
of solar surface velocity), continuum filtergrams (broad-wavelength
photographs of the solar photosphere), and both line-of-sight and
vector magnetograms (maps of the photospheric magnetic field).
