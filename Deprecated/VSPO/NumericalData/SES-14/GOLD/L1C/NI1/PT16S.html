Gold Level 1C Day Disk data

ResourceID
spase://VSPO/NumericalData/SES-14/GOLD/L1C/NI1/PT16S

Description

The Level 1C NI1 data is sampled on a uniform angular grid of 0.15° in both Longitude and Latitude. The data is resampled as follows:
* About 4 scan mirror positions to fill the 0.15° L1C bin.
* Use center of L1B and L1C bins to resample (nearest neighbor) GOLD Public Science Data Products Guide (1.0) 26
* Spectral data is combined within a L1C bin WITHOUT doing any wavelength alignment
for each L1B sample
* 51 bins E-W
* 100 bins N-S
