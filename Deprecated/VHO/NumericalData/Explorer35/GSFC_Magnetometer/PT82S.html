Explorer 35 81.92s magnetic field data

ResourceID
spase://VHO/NumericalData/Explorer35/GSFC_Magnetometer/PT82S

Description

This data set, from the GSFC magnetometer on the lunar-
orbiting Explorer 35 spacecraft, contains 81.92s averages, taken over
5.12s values, of the magnetic field magnitude and Cartesian components
in GSE and GSM coordinates, plus the standard deviations for field magnitude
and GSE component averages. The spacecraft position vector is given in
GSE, GSM and SSE (Selenocentric Solar Ecliptic) coordinates. Additional
parameters in the data records relate to field data in payload coordinates,
spacecraft spin rate and spin axis direction, relative variability of the
field vector and its magnitude, and various quality flags. The data are
available in annual ASCII files.
